# 1. Introduction

As an electronic engineer, I see Reinforcement Learning (RL) not just as "AI," but as an extension of **Adaptive Control Theory**.

## 1.1 The Bellman Equation

In optimal control, we often deal with the Bellman equation. In RL, it looks like this:

$$
V(s) = \max_a \sum_{s'} P(s'|s,a) [R(s,a,s') + \gamma V(s')]
$$

Where:
* $s$ is the state (State Space)
* $a$ is the action (Control Input)
* $\gamma$ is the discount factor
